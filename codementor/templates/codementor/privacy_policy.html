Codementor Integrator Privacy Policy

Collection of Information

Codementor Integrator handles webhooks from Codementor and adds events to Google Calendar. It stores the raw webhook data and the data for the Google Calendar events it creates.

Codementor Integrator also provides an option to add sets of events to Google Calendar. It accesses the list of calendars available on Google Calendar.

No information is shared with other entities.
