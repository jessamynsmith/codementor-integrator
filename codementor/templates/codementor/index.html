What is Codementor Integrator?

Codementor Integrator makes it easier to manage your Codementor Sessions on your Google Calendar.

You can set your Codementor webhook URL to your Integrator API URL and it will add your sessions to Google Calendar for you. The integrator also provides a form to bulk add sessions to Google Calendar.

Codementor Integrator is not affiliated with any other organization, nor does it share your data. You can delete your account at any time.
